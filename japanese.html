Japan Colonial Period

Japan's colonization of the Philippines lasted from 1942 to
1945,during World War II, Here are some key events and details during this period

Japan launched a suprise attack on the Philippines on December 1941, just hours after the attack Pearl harbor.The combined forces of American and Filipino troops were initially overwhelmed, leading to the surrender 76,000 defenders in Bataan on April 1942.The infamous death march resulted in the deaths of 7,000 to 10,000 prisoners due to harsh treatment.

The Japanese government promote their culture, suppressing Filipino identity and traditions. Japan also exploited the Philippines' resources, leading to poverty and famine.Filipinos were forced into labor, and many were tortured,or killed. The occupation resulted in an estimated 500,000 deaths.
